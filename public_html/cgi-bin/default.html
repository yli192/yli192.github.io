Ye Li




I am entering my third year as a Ph.D student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Johns Hopkins University. My advisor is Eric Frey at the Division of Medical Imaging Physics. My office is located at Johns Hopkins Outpatient Center 4250.

I graduated in December 2013 from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a B.S. in Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering and a minor in Physics. My undergraduate research advisor is Rizwan Uddin.

I can be reached by email at yli192@jhu.edu.

Projects
